#Welcome to Night Penguin!
Night Penguin is a 3D printed penguin that sticks netx to my light switch at my bedroom with extended cables that will lead to stick next to my bed

#You might wonder why make such thing if its will be the same as a light switch?
Funny thing, i am quite scared of the dark. The distance between the light switch at my room with my bed is abit of an effort (as someone that is abit scared of the dark). also, I've always wanted to have a light switch LITERALLY next to my bed, so I can turn the lights off while comfortably laying in bed. Therefore, i invented the Night-Penguin!

#How does it works?
It simply works as a regular light switch (for now) with extended cables and a cute custom 3D printed Penguin wheres its arms are basically the light switches!

#Components I use (Version 1) :
- 1 Arduino Nano (Microcontroller)
- 1 Medium Bread Board
- 2 Push Buttons
- 2 Micro Servos
- Male to Male Jumper Wires (as needed)
- Male to Female Jumper Wires (as needed)
- Some Very Long Cables (as needed with the distance from your light swich to your bed)
- Type C Cable (to transfer the code from laptop to Nano)
  Note: you can use any microcontrollers*
